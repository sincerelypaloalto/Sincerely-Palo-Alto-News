About Us

We are a team of two passionate student journalists from the Bay Area, dedicated to changing the way teenagers around the world access trustworthy news.

Our mission is to build a newspaper that is relevant everyone -- professors, students, parents alike -- by presenting information clearly.

Because we are not part of any school, we welcome contributors from anyone with an interest in journalism, whether it is writing, photography, or regardless of your expertise.

We are grateful to our readers and contributors that spend their time reading and writing, helping us foster a community around the Bay Area.
